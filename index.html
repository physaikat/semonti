Us

Every love story is beautiful. But my favorite is ours! It started in a summer of 2003. Back then it was a pure friendship. Isn’t it beautiful when two buddies decide to be best friends forever? A challenge was taken in 2005 for walking along together and today after a decade they are going to become life partner of each other. Come and join us in this most awaited moment of our life, to celebrate our togetherness.
